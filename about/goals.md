# 🎯 Goals

> Where I'm heading — and what I'm building toward.

---

## 🤖 Automation — Going Deeper

Automation is my core focus. I've already built and released several automation tools under the **Mr-SxR** brand, but I'm just getting started.

My goal is to release a few larger, more ambitious automation projects — tools that handle complex workflows, run reliably at scale, and actually make a difference for the people using them. I want Mr-SxR to be known as a source of software that works — not just "good enough", but genuinely well-built.

---

## 🌐 Web Development — Keep Building

I want to keep growing in web development — not just maintaining what I know, but actively working on projects that push me to learn more. Backend architecture, API design, database optimization — these are areas I want to go deeper into.

The goal isn't just to know the tools. It's to be able to build complete, production-ready web applications from scratch.

---

## 🔍 Reverse Engineering — Keep Exploring

Reverse engineering is something I enjoy deeply. Understanding how apps work at a level most people never see — that's still exciting to me. I plan to keep sharpening this skill, exploring new techniques, and applying it to real analysis work.

---

## 📦 Mr-SxR — Building a Brand

Every project I release carries the **Mr-SxR** name. My goal is to make that name mean something — a mark of quality, reliability, and purposeful software. Not dozens of half-finished projects, but fewer things done properly.

---

## 🧠 The Bigger Picture

Keep learning. Keep building. Keep shipping.

I'm self-taught and I intend to stay that way — curiosity-driven, hands-on, and always working on something. The goal was never a title or a certificate. It was always just to build things that work.
